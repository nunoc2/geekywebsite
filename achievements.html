Personal achievements

Throughout his glorious career, Cristiano Ronaldo has achieved numerous personal accolades, including Ballon d'Or (5): 2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017;
FIFA World Player of the Year/The Best (3): 2008, 2016, 2017;
Golden Boots: Winner of 4 European Golden Boots;
UEFA Best Player: Record holder with 3 awards;
All-time Top Scorer: He is the all-time leading scorer in official matches in football history, having surpassed the mark of 960 official goals for clubs and national teams (until mid-2025/2026);
Top Scorer for National Teams:
Holds the record for most goals for a national team (143 goals for Portugal);
Most International Appearances: Record holder for most international matches (226);
Mister Champions: League winner: Top scorer (140 goals) and assist provider (42) in the Champions League.
History of the UEFA Champions League, European Championship:Top scorer (14 goals) and assist provider (8) in the history of the European Championship:
Club Consistency: Only player to score 100+ goals for four different clubs (Sporting, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus).
