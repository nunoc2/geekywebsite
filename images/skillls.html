Skills and Goals

Goals

Cristiano Ronaldo is a goal collector these days, having scored 961 goals throughout his career, making him the all-time leading scorer. The Portuguese player can score goals in all sorts of ways: weak foot, knee, shoulder, good foot, and his specialty, with his head. Passes

Ronaldo, besides being a great shooter, is also a great assist provider, being the top assist provider for the Portuguese national team.
